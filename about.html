About Us

We are the creaters of TL;DR.

It's hard for students with learning disabilities such as ADHD and dyslexia to be able to maintain their focus for an extended period of time whilst reading and comprehending large bodies of text. That's why we created TL;DR - an online text summariser that utilizes accessible fonts to take a large body of text and return a shorter paragraph that concisely summarises the text.
